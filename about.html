About

Welcome to Obelix Interiors, an international full-service interior design company. Since 2015, we've been creating stunning, functional spaces for clients around the world. We work closely with our
clients to understand their needs, preferences, and lifestyles, and to develop personalized
design solutions that reflect their unique vision and style. At Obelix Interiors, we're committed to
sustainability and environmentally responsible design, and we use eco-friendly materials and practices whenever possible. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and discover how we can
help you transform your space into a beautiful, functional, and sustainable environment.
